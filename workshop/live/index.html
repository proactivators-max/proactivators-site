The Agenda

FOUR HOURS. ONE RESET.

Hour 01

MASTERING YOUR TIME

We open with the Quadrant of Time Management — but not the one on a corporate poster. You audit your actual week. You see, in numbers, where your hours are going. The illusion of busy gets dismantled in real time.

Hour 02

THE PARADIGM SHIFT

I show you something you think you're seeing — and prove you're seeing it wrong. The "Old Lady or Young Woman?" illusion becomes the doorway into a deeper truth: most performance problems aren't skill problems. They're paradigm problems.

Hour 03

THE HABIT LOOP

We expose the architecture of every habit you have — Cue, Craving, Routine, Reward. Then we identify your keystone habits: the foundational behaviors that change everything else. You leave with a 30-day installation plan for one specific habit.

Hour 04

THE MASTERMIND ROUND

Mindset becomes accountability. The room breaks into mastermind tables of 5. Each person surfaces the area of their life that's drifting hardest right now. No fixing. No rescuing. Just the truth, told to you by four people who don't owe you anything except honesty. The hour closes with a signed 30-day commitment and an accountability partner.
