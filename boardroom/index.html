The Room

WHO IT'S FOR.

The Boardroom is for ProActivators who have outgrown self-help.

You've read the books. You've done the workshops. You've signed the commitments. What you've never had is a room — a real room — where you show up monthly, in front of 10 to 12 people who are operating at your level, and tell the truth about where you're drifting.

This is that room.

  • Founders and senior operators carrying the weight of their organization
  • Sales leaders, executives, and high-performers who plateau quietly
  • Pastors, ministry directors, and faith-leaders who lead others while drifting themselves
  • Recovery-walking professionals rebuilding a life of purpose
  • Anyone tired of operating below their capacity — and ready to be in a room with others who feel the same

This is a room for leaders. Not for any one demographic, profession, or background. The only entry requirements are honesty, commitment, and the willingness to be sharpened.

The Code

GROUND RULES.

  1. Confidentiality is absolute. What's said in The Boardroom stays here.
  2. Maximum 2 missed sessions per quarter. 3+ in a year and you lose your seat.
  3. Webcam on. No phones. No driving. No multitasking.
  4. You bring your real numbers. Pillar scores aren't a brag list — they're the truth.
  5. No fixing. No rescuing. We ask questions. We tell the truth. We don't perform.
  6. Contribution-first. You are not in this room to extract. You are here to sharpen and be sharpened.
  7. The drift ends here.
Once Per Year

THE ANNUAL RETREAT.

Once per year, members of The Boardroom gather in person — the day before the public ProActivators Summit.

It's a private, full-day intensive: deep-work sessions, 1:1 time with Gabriel, and a final commitment ceremony to close out the year. Then the Summit doors open and you walk into the public event already aligned, already accountable, already ready to lead.

Your Summit ticket is included with your membership. So is VIP seating.
